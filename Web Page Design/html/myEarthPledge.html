"What can we do to save the world?"

Three Rs
Conserve Energy
Substainable Transport
Conserve Wild Life
Reduce, Reuse and Recycle

Reduce
We should and we can reduce the wastes, use less water bottles and bring our own bags while shopping.
Reuse
We should find ways to reuse the things. For example, we could donate or share our clothes we don't want to use to the charity organization. This also save the carbon dioxide(CO2) emission.
Recycle
We should dispose of our products which are recyclable products into the recycle bin, This, in turn, enhances the substainability.

Substainable Transport

Driving our own vehicles produce a lot of carbon dioxide, a gas which is responsible for green house effect. Green house effect,in turn, is responsible for sea-level rising. This is a dangerous environmental issue, especially for the countries like lowest-lying countries, like Singapore. 30 per cent of all land in Singapore is less than 5m above sea level.(Straittimes) So,we need to reduce using our own mobile vehicles and try to use public transport.

Conserve Energy

We produce most of the energy from the burning of fossil fuels. So, we should conserve energy by taking the following actions.

  • Disconnecting the lamps, and kitchen appliances, and other electrical devices when they are not in use
  • Using LED lighting because they consume less energy
  • Only using your dishwasher or washing machine when the load is full
Caring and Maintaining Wild life

Biodiversity is really important for both humans and all the living things on the earth. We, all the species, are depending on each other. For example, one third of the world's crop production is attributed to pollinators like birds, bees, and other insects. As indiviuduals, we can achieve this by eating organic food which do not use fertilizers and insecticides,participating and suppoprting activites and organizations which encourages to maintain biodiversity.
